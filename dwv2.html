No 1 About Project

Come Deliwin With Us

Deliwin v.2 is an updated version of my cafe website that was built as a template. Its features now include a user friendly checkout, a filterable menu, convenient food reorders, and a helpful map of cafes. This website was given an award for "Best Solution for a Business."

No 2 The Process

Starting Out

I took the design I had originally made and went off of that. With my experience in greensock, I had made the animations a lot smoother and complex. I worked with making the website mobile responsive and it will look best on an iPhone 6 and above.


Half-Way Point

I was setting up most of the endpoints to work with my database so that I could quickly move through the design of the website. I made navigation through the page smooth and easy to work with. I created a "smart" cart that lets you easily add, remove, order, and reorder food. I had created most of the visual aspects and logic of the website, as well as the backend endpoints to easily access all of the needed data.


Finishing Up

I began to focus on the mobile design and the responsiveness of the website. I finished setting up the last couple of endpoints for the user's information. I fixed bugs that popped up and made any last couple of changes that were needed.
