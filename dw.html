No 1 About Project

Come Deliwin With Us

Deliwin is a cafe website that was built as a personally made template. Its design allows for users to easily navigate the website and find information about the cafe, search for local cafes, view a filterable online menu, and place online orders. This website was given an award for "Best Solution for a Business."

No 2 The Process

Starting Out

This personal project is only a template of a cafe website. Deliwin Cafe was actually an idea created by me and a friend in high school. We liked the idea of mixing Hispanic and Vietnamese foods and serving drinks with them. I had to design how the website would look, and how other potential restaurants could use the templates.


Half-Way Point

I was working mainly on the logic for this website. I had to implement the navbar to work with the individual routes. I had to keep track of what users had in their cart throughout their entire visit. I was focusing mainly on the front-end so that I could set up the endpoints and begin back-end development.


Finishing Up

by the end of the second week, I was mainly finishing up the back-end. I was also implementing more features such as stripe, greensock, and parallax. I had reached my mvp by the end of the week and was ready to present my project.
